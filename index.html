AI and Analytics S&P Global (via IHS Markit merger)

Delivered 83% accuracy on natural-language portfolio queries for 300+ enterprise users using a fine-tuned in-house LLM

Investment teams needed natural-language access to portfolio data, but deploying a frontier LLM off-the-shelf posed regulatory language and institutional tone risks. Made the strategic decision to integrate Spark Assist — a fine-tuned in-house LLM trained on S&P tone, behavior, and proprietary financial knowledge — rather than use a frontier model, achieving significant cost advantage and domain precision. Defined the production-readiness evaluation framework with real query test cases from SMEs and friendly clients, synthetic red-team datasets, and behavioral guardrails. Implemented authorization-before-retrieval architecture to enforce access control at the retrieval layer before any LLM call.

83% accuracy 300+ enterprise users Fine-tuned in-house LLM Enterprise compliance architecture
Compliance and Security S&P Global (via IHS Markit merger)

Blocked a multi-product rollout to protect 100% GDPR compliance across EU clients

A planned unified suite rollout was viable in the US but would have violated GDPR data residency requirements for EU clients by co-mingling US and EU data on shared infrastructure. Authored the trade-off decision document and RAID log escalated to executive leadership, making the case to halt the rollout until regional server separation was implemented. Defined the architectural requirements for US/EU separation, worked with engineering to confirm compliance across all EU client data, and established the governance gate before any further platform investment was approved.
